About the Competition

InnoCode Camp 2024 is not just a competition; it's a journey to push the boundaries of your programming skills. Organized by the CF Department in collaboration with the International Cooperation & Personal Development Office at FPT University Danang, this is the perfect arena to shine on the tech map!

In the qualifying round of the web design competition, Teams will come up with ideas and base them on the front-end platform; Each Team will need to design the main interface and subpages inside, focusing on interface design without requiring functional development. Evaluation criteria include aesthetics, layout, creativity, consistency, and presentation skills.
In particular, the organizers hope to see unique and creative ideas expressed through each design, along with the harmonious use of colors, fonts, and images.
This contest is not only an opportunity for you to show off your design talent but also an opportunity to learn, interact and challenge yourself in a professional environment. Wishing the Teams a productive time and success in the competition!
Number of members: 3 to 5 students/Team
