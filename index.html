About the Competition

InnoCode Camp 2024 is not just a competition; it's a journey to push the boundaries of your programming skills. Organized by the CF Department in collaboration with the International Cooperation & Personal Development Office at FPT University Danang, this is the perfect arena to shine on the tech map!

In the qualifying round of the web design competition, Teams will come up with ideas and base them on the front-end platform; Each Team will need to design the main interface and subpages inside, focusing on interface design without requiring functional development. Evaluation criteria include aesthetics, layout, creativity, consistency, and presentation skills.
In particular, the organizers hope to see unique and creative ideas expressed through each design, along with the harmonious use of colors, fonts, and images.
This contest is not only an opportunity for you to show off your design talent but also an opportunity to learn, interact and challenge yourself in a professional environment. Wishing the Teams a productive time and success in the competition!
Number of members: 3 to 5 students/Team

Here are some key details about this event:

1. Prizes and Subsequent Announcements

- Prizes: The InnoCodeCamp promises to offer attractive prizes for the winning teams, aiming to honor their efforts and achievements while also encouraging continuous creativity and improvement. Details about the prizes will be announced in subsequent communications from the organizers.
- Subsequent Announcements: Updates regarding the prizes and related terms will be periodically released to ensure all participants are timely and fully informed.

2. Project Submission and GitHub Pages

- Project Submission: All competition entries must be submitted through GitHub, a popular source code management platform, ensuring transparency and ease of assessment and review.
- GitHub Pages: The use of GitHub Pages not only facilitates easy access and presentation of the projects but also allows participants to professionally showcase their solutions and engage with the community.

3. Use of UI Frameworks

- Tool Support: Participants are encouraged to use modern User Interface (UI) frameworks such as Bootstrap, Angular Material, React or Vue.js to design their user interfaces. This not only improves the aesthetic appeal of the products but also enhances user experience.
- Encouraging Creativity: The organizers encourage the creative use of these tools to maximize the functionality, performance, and user-friendliness of the applications.
The InnoCodeCamp is an ideal platform for computer science students to explore and maximize their potential. The competition emphasizes the importance of programming and software development while also reinforcing skills in teamwork, problem-solving, and innovation.
